For students. For professors. For employers.

Sign In Sign Up

The FDIBA Career Center lets you register as either a student, faculty member or employer.

As a student you can complete your career/academic profile and browse through a wide array of jobs internships. You can apply with ease and get connected with employers lightning fast.
As a faculty member you can create classes and enroll students in them. You can generate reports and easily manage everyone.
As an employer you can create job advertisements easily and fast. Connecting with students has never been easier.
