for students, university faculty and employers

Sign In Sign Up

The FDIBA Career center lets you register as either a student, faculty member or employer.

Students can:

  • Find jobs
  • Find internships

Faculty members can:

  • Create classes
  • Enrol students in classes
  • Update transcripts of students

Employers can:

  • Create job/internship ads
  • Create questionnaires
